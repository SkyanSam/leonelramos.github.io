This is my blog. I like to write about anything that interests me therefore I have added a filter so you can filter out any topics that don't interest you. I made this blog mostly for myself, it could prove useful one day to have my thoughts documented. Regardless of its purpose, if my blog can help solve some of your problems by reading solutions to my own then I am content it has taken on another purpose.

Oh and if you are wondering who Jim Thorpe is, wonder no further, for it is I. It is a stupid inside joke between my girlfriend and I. She got me a coffee mug that reads "Jim Thorpe" from the town Jim Thorpe. I joked that she had forgotten my name while buying me the souvenir. That she was faced with two mugs to choose from, one with my name on it and one with "Jim Thorpe". After some internal-struggle and deliberation she came to the conclusion that my name was indeed Jim Thorpe and not Leonel Ramos and purchased the Jim Thorpe mug for me. So yea ... I've been Jim Thrope ever since.
